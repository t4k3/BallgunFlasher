Update Guide

1
Connect DRIVER USB

2
Update DRIVER
Click Connect, then Update DRIVER
3
Connect DISPLAY USB
Disconnect DRIVER, plug USB to ESP32 at bottom (yellow box)
4
Update DISPLAY
Click Connect, then Update DISPLAY
5
Update Nextion (optional)
Keep DISPLAY USB connected, select .tft file and click Update
6
Done!
